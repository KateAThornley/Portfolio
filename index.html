My Work

Figma Designs

Through the use of online tools like Figma, I make high fidelity wireframes for various school, client, and personal projects. Designing mockups allows me to better my understanding of design fundamentals like whitespace, visual hierarchy, consistency, and scale. Check out my 1st Figma draft for a portfolio site vs my final draft here!

Application Development

For 8 months I worked full-time on developing the front-end of a job-searching application for the Government of Canada. I worked primarily in JavaScript with a React framework, and HTML/CSS. During sprints I would develop, implement and adjust my mockups to find what worked best for our clients while also meeting WCAG 2.0 AA accessibility standards.

Merging Arts and CS

I’m a firm believer of the STEAM movement, and as such I’m always trying to blend the arts into my CS projects. Thus, using Python’s Spacy, my friend Humam Shwaikh and I created an ML program that performs basic sentiment analysis on literature, in order to show how computing can be used to better understand art.

  • UI/UX Design

About Me

I’m a fourth year Computer Science student, minoring in Digital Humanities at the University of Ottawa, in Ontario, Canada! In my free time I’m usually making some form of art, from music to comics- I have a great respect for anything creative.

Being able to merge my passions for creativity and aesthetics into my future as a developer has been an exciting journey. I’m eager to keep learning through online resources, university courses, personal projects, and possibly working with you!
